Answers

Three primary OOP relationships

  • Aggregation (aka association) The "has-a" relationship.
  • Composition The "is-a-part-of" relationship.
  • Inheritance The "is-a" relationship.

Work-around for implementing many-to-many relationships

Use an association class. This will be translated to a junction table in the database.
